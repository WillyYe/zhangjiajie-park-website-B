A 7.5km pristine stream winding through towering pillars. Crystal-clear waters and ancient trees create one of Zhangjiajie's most enchanting natural corridors.

全长7.5公里的原始溪流，穿行于高耸的石柱之间。清澈的溪水和古老的树木构成了张家界最迷人的自然走廊之一。

About Golden Whip Stream

景区简介

Golden Whip Stream (Jinbianxi) is one of the most treasured natural attractions in Zhangjiajie National Forest Park. The 7.5-kilometer stream flows from Tudi Ya through the heart of the forest, winding between thousands of towering quartz sandstone pillars before reaching Shui Rao Si Men (Water Running Four Doors).

金鞭溪是张家界国家森林公园最珍贵的自然景观之一。7.5公里长的溪流从土地垭发源，穿行于万峰耸立的石英砂岩之间，最终流至水绕四门。

The stream gets its name from Jinbian Rock (Golden Whip Rock), a 380-meter tall golden-colored pillar that resembles an ancient whip. The area is renowned as a "natural oxygen bar" with负氧离子浓度高达每立方厘米10万个，是城市的100多倍。

溪流因金鞭岩而得名——这是一座高380米、金黄色的石柱，形似古代鞭子。该地区被誉为"天然氧吧"，空气中负氧离子含量极高，是城市的100多倍。

Key Features

主要特色

  • 🏔️ Golden Whip Rock金鞭岩

    A 380-meter tall golden pillar resembling an ancient whip. Named after a legendary golden whip left by a celestial soldier.

    高380米的金色石柱，形似古代鞭子。传说因天兵遗落金鞭而得名。

  • 🌸 Thousand-Year-Old Ginkgo千年古银杏

    Ancient ginkgo trees over 500 years old stand along the stream, their golden autumn foliage painting the landscape.

    沿溪生长的500年以上古银杏树，秋日金黄的落叶装点山川。

  • 🦜 Wildlife Haven野生动物家园

    Home to over 200 bird species and numerous small mammals. Lucky visitors might spot golden snub-nosed monkeys.

    栖息着200多种鸟类和众多小型哺乳动物，幸运的游客可能看到金丝猴。

  • 💎 Crystal Waters清澈溪水

    Pristine stream water so clear you can see fish swimming at the bottom. Perfect for cooling your feet on hot summer days.

    清澈见底的溪流，可以看到鱼儿在水中游弋。炎炎夏日还可以在溪中戏水解暑。

💡 Travel Tips

💡 游览贴士

  • Start early (6:30-8:00) for a peaceful experience before tour groups arrive
  • Wear water-resistant shoes - the path can be slippery near the water
  • Bring insect repellent, especially during summer months
  • The full hike from entrance to Water Running Four Doors takes 2-3 hours
  • Combine with Yuanjiajie by taking the Bailong Elevator back up
  • 建议早起出发（6:30-8:00），在旅行团到达前享受宁静
  • 穿着防滑防水的鞋子，溪边路面较湿滑
  • 夏季记得带驱蚊液
  • 从入口徒步至水绕四门全程约需2-3小时
  • 可与袁家界联游，从百龙天梯返回山顶
