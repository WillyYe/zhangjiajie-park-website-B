Transportation Details

✈

By Plane

Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport (DYG)
35km to park entrance (~45 min by taxi).
Flights from Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, and other major cities.

Taxi: ¥100-150 Shuttle: ¥30
🚄

By High-Speed Train

Zhangjiajie West Station (high-speed rail)
20km to park entrance (~30 min by taxi).
Direct trains from Changsha (2.5h), Guangzhou (5h), Beijing (8h).

Taxi: ¥60-80
🚌

By Bus

Zhangjiajie Bus Station
Direct buses to the park run every 10-20 minutes.
Duration: ~40 minutes. Show the driver: “国家森林公园”

Bus: ¥15
🚗

By Car / Taxi

Navigate to the park ticket station.
Parking available at the entrance (~¥20/day).
GPS: 29.3256, 110.4348

Parking: ¥20/day
