Tianzi Mountain

天子山

At 1,262m, the king of all viewpoints in Zhangjiajie. Witness spectacular sunrises, sea of clouds, and panoramic pillar landscapes.

海拔1262米，张家界众山之巅。观赏壮丽日出、云海翻涌，俯瞰万千石峰。

About Tianzi Mountain

景区简介

Tianzi Mountain (Emperor Mountain) is named after Xiang DaKun, a legendary leader of local Tujia ethnic minorities who led an uprising during the early Ming Dynasty and proclaimed himself "Heavenly Son" (Tianzi). At 1,262 meters above sea level, it stands as the highest peak in the Wulingyuan scenic area and commands sweeping panoramic views of the surrounding pillar landscape.

天子山因明代土家族农民起义领袖向大坤自称"天子"而得名。海拔1262米，是武陵源景区最高峰，可俯瞰周围壮观的石峰景观。

The mountain is famous for its saying: "Expanded Bonsai, Miniature Fairyland" (扩大的盆景，缩小的仙境) - referring to how the pillar formations below resemble miniature landscapes viewed from above. The area encompasses the famous Imperial Brush Peak (御笔峰) and General's Hall (将军岩), among other iconic formations.

景区以"扩大的盆景，缩小的仙境"著称——从山顶俯瞰，下方石峰如微缩景观。天子山包含著名的御笔峰、将军岩等标志性景观。

🌄 Best Time for Sunrise Viewing: 5:30 - 7:00 AM

🌄 日出观赏最佳时间：清晨 5:30 - 7:00

Top Viewpoints

主要观景点

  • 1

    Imperial Brush Peak (御笔峰)

    御笔峰

    A cluster of six 400-meter tall pillars resembling ancient writing brushes. This is Tianzi Mountain's most iconic formation and a symbol of Wulingyuan.

    六座高400米的石柱群，形似古代毛笔，是天子山的标志性景观，也是武陵源的象征。

  • 2

    General's Rock (将军岩)

    将军岩

    A rock formation resembling a general in military uniform. Legend says this is the petrified General Xiang who protects the mountain.

    形似身着军装的将军的岩石。传说这是化为岩石守护此山的向大坤将军。

  • 3

    He Long Park (贺龙公园)

    贺龙公园

    A memorial park dedicated to Marshal He Long with a bronze statue. Offers excellent views and cultural significance.

    为纪念贺龙元帅而建的公园，园内有贺龙铜像，景色优美且具文化意义。

  • 4

    Skyward Peak (云青岩)

    云青岩

    Named for the clouds that often envelop this peak, creating ethereal sea-of-clouds views that stretch to the horizon.

    因常有云雾缭绕此峰而得名，可欣赏延伸到天际的壮观云海。

💡 Travel Tips

💡 游览贴士

  • Sunrise: Arrive by 5:30 AM at the latest during summer; cableway opens at 6:30
  • Cableway: Take the Tianzi Mountain Cableway from Wulingyuan (40 RMB one-way)
  • Weather: Check the weather forecast - cloudy days offer the best sea-of-clouds views
  • Clothing: Bring a light jacket even in summer - the summit can be cool
  • Alternative: Hikers can climb from Yangjiajie (3-4 hours)
  • 日出：夏季最迟5:30前到达，索道6:30开放
  • 索道：从武陵源乘坐天子山索道上山（单程40元）
  • 天气：查看天气预报——多云天最适合观赏云海
  • 衣物：即使夏季也建议带薄外套，山顶较凉爽
  • 徒步：也可从杨家界徒步上山（需3-4小时）

Getting to Tianzi Mountain

交通方式

The most convenient way to reach Tianzi Mountain is via the Tianzi Mountain Cableway from Wulingyuan Direction. The 45-minute cableway ride offers stunning aerial views of the pillar landscape. Alternatively, hikers can trek from Yangjiajie, a challenging 3-4 hour climb through pristine forest.

前往天子山最便捷的方式是从武陵源方向乘坐天子山索道。45分钟的索道之旅可欣赏壮观的空中石峰景观。也可从杨家界徒步登山，穿越原始森林约需3-4小时。
