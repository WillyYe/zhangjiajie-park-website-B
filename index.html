Getting Here

Multiple ways to reach Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

🗺️
加载地图中...
✈️

By Plane

Zhangjiajie Hehua International Airport, about 35km from the park, with flights to major Chinese cities.

🚄

By Train

Zhangjiajie Station and Zhangjiajie West Station (high-speed), about 20km from the park.

🚌

By Bus

Direct buses from Zhangjiajie Bus Station, about 40 minutes, approximately ¥15.

🚗

By Car

Navigate to Zhangjiajie National Forest Park Ticket Station, parking available.
